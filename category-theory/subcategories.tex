    A \textit{subcategory} of a \ex{Categories}{category} $C$ is another \ex{Categories}{category} $B$ formed from a \ex{Subsets}{subset} of the objects and morphisms of $C$ with the same \ex{CompositeFunctions}{composition} and identities as $C$.

    \begin{definition}[Full \textit{Subcategories}]
        A \textit{subcategory} $B$ of $C$ is \textbf{full} iff $\forall x,y \in B, B(x,y) = C(x,y)$
        \textit{i.e. only orphaned morphisms are removed.}
    \end{definition}
